PDf Compacter 2002-10-03 Karl Podlesak Source

Requirements:
Acrobat PDF Writer 4.05

Execute the program PDFCompacter.exe
Click on Compact a PDF
Select the PDF Document
Click on Open or press enter/return

The program will automatically compact the document to it's most compressed state, while in a PDF.  If the program couldn't compress the PDF document, it will prompt you?

To Exit
Click on the Exit button
